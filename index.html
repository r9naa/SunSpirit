Chemical Reaction Process

Our technology utilizes a two-step chemical reaction process. In the first process, artificial photosynthesis occurs where CO₂, O₂, and sunlight are taken in, and oxygen is released. In the second process, the carbon from the first reaction is carried into the second chamber where it reacts with titanium dioxide, resulting in the creation of CH₄ (methane).

NDIR Non-Dispersive Infrared Sensors
